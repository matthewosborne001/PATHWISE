PATHWISE Calculator

How This Calculator Works

PathWise helps you both track past performance and plan future results.

  • Current Average – Your overall grade right now.
  • Target Grade – The final grade you want to achieve.
  • Grade – The score you received on an assessment.
  • Weight % – How much that assessment counts toward your final grade.

Add completed assessments to calculate your projected average. Use your target grade to understand whether you are on track and what performance level you need moving forward.
