What PathWise Does

PathWise helps students stop reacting to deadlines and start planning ahead. Enter your subject, deadline, and difficulty level, and the system calculates when you should begin and how much you should study each day.

The free version focuses on smarter deadline starts. PathWise Plus expands this into a full academic control dashboard with workload balancing, long-term planning, and advanced insights.
