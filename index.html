What PathWise Does

PathWise helps students stop reacting to deadlines and start planning ahead. Instead of last-minute panic, you enter your subject, deadline, and difficulty, and the system calculates when you should begin and how much to study daily.

The free version focuses on structured single-assessment planning. PathWise Plus expands this into a full Academic Control Dashboard with workload balancing, deadline collision detection, risk alerts, and long-term academic forecasting.
