Why PathWise?

Academic pressure builds silently. Deadlines approach faster than expected, workload feels heavier than it looks, and starting too late becomes a pattern. PathWise helps you take control before stress takes control of you. By entering your subject, deadline, and difficulty level, PathWise calculates how much work is realistically required and when you should begin. Instead of guessing when to start or underestimating the effort needed, you receive a structured starting plan that keeps you ahead, organised, and intentional.
The free version helps you start correctly. PathWise Plus helps you control everything. With Plus, you unlock the full Academic Control Dashboard — manage multiple subjects at once, detect deadline collisions, measure weekly workload intensity, and receive risk alerts before overload happens. Instead of reacting to pressure, you plan around it. If you want clarity across your entire academic schedule — not just one assessment — PathWise Plus turns planning into strategy..
