What PathWise Does

PathWise helps students stop reacting to deadlines and start planning ahead. Instead of last-minute panic, you enter your subject, deadline, and difficulty level, and the system calculates when you should begin and how much you should study each day.

The free version focuses on smarter deadline starts and structured single-assessment planning. PathWise Plus expands this into a full Academic Control Dashboard with workload balancing, deadline collision detection, risk alerts, and long-term academic forecasting.
