What PathWise Does

PathWise helps students stop reacting to deadlines and start planning ahead. Instead of cramming the night before or underestimating how long something will take, PathWise introduces structured preparation.
You enter your subject, deadline, and difficulty level. The system calculates when you should begin and how much you should study daily — turning pressure into a controlled plan.

Why Consider PathWise Plus

The free version focuses on smarter deadline starts. It gives clarity for individual assessments and reduces last-minute stress.
PathWise Plus expands this into a full academic control dashboard: multiple assessments, workload balancing, collision detection, risk alerts, and long-term planning insights.
